## Spoiler Free definition
The sumo matches take place in the evening Japan time, so very early morning US time. Rarely are we able to watch live. Youtube has a daily summary of the matched that is delayed by one or more days. Depending on the player, we sometimes get multiple days behind the actual tournament. So a user may want to, for example, see the results up to day 4 even though results are available up to day 10. Upon logging in, there should be a menu that allows a given user to select up to what day he wants to limit results.
